Learning Management System

An engaging platform for online education and skill development

Role Senior UX Designer
Timeline 14 weeks
Team 5 members
Tools Figma, Miro, HTML, CSS
View Live Demo

Project Overview

LearnHub is a comprehensive learning management system designed to facilitate online education for students, educators, and organizations. The platform combines course delivery, progress tracking, and community features to create an engaging learning environment.

The Challenge

Traditional LMS platforms often suffer from poor user experience, outdated interfaces, and lack of engagement features. Our challenge was to create a modern platform that would:

  • Engage learners through intuitive and motivating interfaces
  • Support various learning styles and content types
  • Provide comprehensive progress tracking and analytics
  • Facilitate instructor-student and peer-to-peer interaction
  • Scale from individual learners to large organizations

The Solution

I designed a learner-centric platform that gamifies education while providing powerful tools for instructors and administrators. The interface emphasizes progress visualization and community engagement.

Interactive Learning

Multimedia content delivery with interactive exercises, quizzes, and hands-on assignments.

Progress Tracking

Comprehensive analytics and progress visualization to keep learners motivated and on track.

Collaborative Learning

Discussion forums, group projects, and peer review systems that build learning communities.

Achievement System

Gamification elements including badges, certificates, and learning streaks to motivate continued engagement.

Key Features

Personalized Learning Dashboard

The main dashboard provides a personalized learning experience with course recommendations, progress tracking, and upcoming assignments. The interface adapts to individual learning patterns and preferences.

  • Personalized course recommendations
  • Progress visualization and goals
  • Upcoming deadlines and schedules
  • Achievement and streak tracking

Comprehensive Course Experience

Course pages provide rich multimedia content with interactive elements, progress tracking, and social features. The design supports various content types while maintaining consistent navigation and user experience.

  • Multimedia content delivery
  • Interactive assignments and quizzes
  • Discussion and collaboration tools
  • Offline content access

Analytics and Insights

Detailed analytics help both learners and instructors understand learning patterns, identify areas for improvement, and track progress toward educational goals. Data visualization makes complex information accessible.

  • Learning progress analytics
  • Performance comparisons
  • Time spent and engagement metrics
  • Predictive learning insights

Results & Impact

The learning management system design significantly improved educational outcomes and user engagement:

73%
