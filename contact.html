Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A typical website redesign takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex applications can take 8-12 weeks. I can also help in urgent cases But, I always provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely! I love working with startups and early-stage companies. I understand the unique challenges and constraints that come with building something new, and I'm experienced in creating scalable design systems.

What's included in your design process?

My process typically includes user research, wireframing, visual design, prototyping, and design handoff. Good news for you, I also provide ongoing support during development and can assist with user testing and iterations.

Do you offer development services?

While I focus primarily on design, I can handle frontend development for smaller projects. For larger applications, I prefer to collaborate with my development teams and provide detailed design specifications and support.
